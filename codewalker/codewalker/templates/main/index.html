PY102 : Vskill Certified Python Developer

Get certified and increase your chances to get hired.

In this course we will prepare you for Vskill Certified Python Developer. It is a certification program by Vskills, which is a Govt. of India & Govt. of NCT Delhi and JC Co. Initiative. This is the only certification available from a known authority, all others are fake or non-existent or of no value. There is no official Python certification program as well. Official certifications are the most difficult and have the best value in the market. We are waiting for the python.org foundation to launch a certification soon.

Here we will strictly follow the certification syllabus available from Vskills.
